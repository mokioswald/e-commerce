Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

At Premier Online, we are committed to delivering your orders quickly and safely. Here are the key points of our shipping policy:

  • Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
  • We offer standard and express shipping options.
  • Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your location.
  • Free shipping is available for orders above a specified amount.

Return Policy

We aim to ensure customer satisfaction. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return your items under these conditions:

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase.
  • The items should be unused and in their original packaging.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after receiving the returned items.
  • Return shipping costs may apply unless the item is defective or damaged.
